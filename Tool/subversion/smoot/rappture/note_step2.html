Initial Observations

In this step, enter the initial observations of the system you wish to evaluate in the following fields:

  • Observed Inputs and Observed Outputs: Upload your observations in the upper loader menu. Upload observed inputs and outputs from CSV files into the designated fields respectively.
  • Lower Input Bounds and Upper Input Bounds: Turn on the Input Bounds toggle control and enter the lower and upper bounds of the inputs in a tuple form (e.g. "0,0" and 1,1" for 2 variables, both with the lower bound of zero and the upper bound of one). Also, you may opt to set the toggle control to "off" and upload a set of desired designs of your experiment in a CSV file in the upper loader menu, along with your observations.
  • Number of Iterations: Enter the maximum number of iterations for the program
  • See the user manual to get information related to the data upload and the correct CSV file formatting.

After entering all inputs, proceed to the next step.
